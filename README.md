## What is a 1-Channel Phone RS-485 Fiber Converter?

A 1-channel phone RS-485 fiber converter converts RS-485 voice and data signals to optical signals for transmission over fiber. This allows long-distance communication beyond the limitations of traditional copper wiring while preserving low latency and data integrity. The converters support both standalone and rack-mounted installations and are commonly used in industrial control rooms, building automation, and telecom environments.
